The Service

Global Creative Innovation brings eight manufacturing companies to offer diversified products in the component and assembly indsutries. Customers include small to big corporations in need of various assembly parts for automotives, firearms, construction, and machinery.


It can be an inconvenience to call separate manufacturers. GCI offers a single point to make all the orders from these manufacturing partners simpler for the product user.


Not only do we sell components. We can customize components, assembly products, and machinery. With molding and die casting technologies from our manufacturing partners we can adjust to your businesses needs.
