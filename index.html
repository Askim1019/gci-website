The Service

Global Creative Innovation connects eight manufacturing companies to offer diversified products in the component and assembly indsutries. Clients include small to big corporations in need of various assembly parts for automotives, firearms, construction, and machinery. GCI is a centralized source to order customizable products that fit the needs of clients. Our manufacturing partners use welding, die casting, shot blasting, and machining technologies to form the components needed for assembling customized parts for cars, trucks, and construction vehicles.
